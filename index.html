About Mystique

In the heart of Ul'dah's entertainment district lies Mystique—an exclusive sanctuary where Eorzea's elite gather for refined pleasures and discreet indulgence. Behind velvet curtains and crystal chandeliers, our establishment offers an escape from the mundane into a world of sophistication and sensuality.

Founded by a former courtesan of the Songbird's court, Mystique has earned its reputation through impeccable service, absolute discretion, and an atmosphere of decadent luxury. Whether you seek stimulating conversation, exquisite performances, or more intimate companionship, our carefully curated staff awaits to fulfill your desires.

House Rules & Etiquette

In-Character Guidelines

  • Respect all staff and patrons at all times
  • Consent is mandatory for all interactions
  • No violence or disruption on premises
  • Formal or semi-formal attire preferred
  • Private services require advance booking

Out-of-Character Policies

  • All participants must be 18+ (player and character)
  • Fade-to-black is always an option
  • No ERP in public spaces
  • Respect boundaries and use proper RP etiquette
  • Zero tolerance for harassment or OOC drama

Important: Mystique is a mature RP venue for adults only. By entering, you acknowledge that you are 18 years or older and consent to adult themes and content. Always communicate boundaries clearly with RP partners.
